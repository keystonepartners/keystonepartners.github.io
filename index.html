Who We Are

Keystone Investment Partners is a student run, hedge fund out of Harvard College. We pursue public markets research and portfolio construction in an investment club format.

  • Approach: research driven, evidence based.
  • Structure: private partnership; decisions by member vote; execution by authorized co-partners.
  • Ethos: humility, transparency, and long term goals

Our Values

Principles that guide every decision.

Integrity

We do what we say, communicate clearly, and earn trust by being consistent and candid.

Research Rigor

Decisions originate from deep, bottom-up analysis and disciplined risk controls.

Alignment

We invest alongside our partners and prioritize outcomes that compound over time.

Risk Management

Protecting capital through scenario analysis, position sizing, and portfolio balance.

Transparency

Clear reporting, repeatable processes, and measurable objectives.

Long-Term Focus

We prefer durable cash flows and structural advantages over short-term noise.
